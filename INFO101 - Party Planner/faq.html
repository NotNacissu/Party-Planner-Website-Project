Frequently Asked Questions

What ages are appropriate for your parties?

Our parties are suitable for children of all ages, from toddlers to teenagers.

How many children can be invited to a party?

We can accommodate parties of various sizes. Please contact us via email or phone to discuss your specific requirements.

What if plans change?

If you need to reschedule or cancel a party, please contact us as soon as possible. We'll work with you to find a suitable solution.

Is coffee available for adults?

Yes, we offer coffee and refreshments for adults attending the party.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept payments via credit cards, PayPal, and bank transfers.

What is your cancellation policy?

If you need to cancel an event, please inform us at least 1 week in advance for a full refund. Cancellation policies may vary for different types of events.

How far in advance should I book your services?

We recommend booking our services at least 1 to 3 months in advance to ensure availability, especially for popular event dates.

Can I customize the decorations for my event?

Absolutely! We work closely with our clients to create a customized decor plan that matches their preferences.

Is parking available at your venue?

Yes, we provide ample parking facilities for our guests. Parking details will be provided upon confirmation of your event booking.

Can I bring my own entertainment for an event?

You are welcome to bring your own entertainment, such as DJs or live bands, with prior arrangements.

Do you offer special accommodations for dietary restrictions?

Yes, we can accommodate various dietary restrictions and preferences. Please let us know in advance, and we'll work with our catering team to ensure that all your guests' needs are met.
