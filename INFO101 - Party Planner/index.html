About Us

We specialize in planning memorable parties for your children. Our company was founded by Wei Zhang, a seasoned professional magician, and Moana Belich, an expert in e-commerce and event management.

At CelebrateMasters, our mission is to provide you with an exceptional party planning experience. We understand that parents and guardians want to create cherished memories for their children without the stress of organizing every detail. That's where we come in.

Our commitment is to offer a efficient planning service for your celebration needs. You select the date and time that suits you best and choose from our curated list of venues. Next, you pick the entertainment that is best suited for your child's needs. To top it all off, we offer a range of catering options to satisfy every customer.

We take pride in creating memorable experiences that will last a lifetime. Thank you for considering us and helping make your child's dreams come true.
